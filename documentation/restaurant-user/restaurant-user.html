User Persona, Use Story, and Interaction Flow

User Persona

  • Name:Dalton Villanueva
  • Age:35
  • Gender:Male
  • Profession:Insurance Agent
  • Technology:iPhone X and 5th generation iPad (with a keyboard)
  • Attitudes:
    • Loves to eat macaroni and cheese
    • Wants to find the best restaurant in Albuquerque for macaroni and cheese
    • Willing to to try new places just for the macaroni and cheese
    • Has eaten at many restaurants in Albuquerque
    • Suggests restaurants to friends and family
    • Willing to eat meals with clients
  • Needs:
    • Needs to wind down after a stressful day at work
    • Needs to be able to compare restaurants easily
    • Needs to learn about restaurants in Albuquerque

User Story:

As a restaurant user, I want to find the best restaurant for mac and cheese in Albuquerque.

Use Case

Dalton is having a lunch meeting with a client to talk over renewing the business insurance policy. He wants to find the best macaroni and cheese restaurant near the client's business location. Dalton opens up the That's What Cheese Said web page to look for all the restaurants. Looking at all of the suggestions, he picks a restaurant that is near the top of the restaurant list and close to the business. Dalton suggests this to the client and they meet there for lunch.

Interaction Flow

Precondition:Dalton is on the homepage website

Postcondition:Dalton has decided where to eat

Interaction Flow:

  • Dalton clicks on Restaurant Ranker
  • Server returns list of restaurants in order of popularity
  • Dalton clicks on the first restaurant to find its address
  • Server returns the restaurant's information including address
  • Dalton is not satisfied with the location of this restaurant, so he returns to the list
  • Server returns list of restaurants in Albuquerque
  • Dalton clicks on the second restaurant in the list.
  • Server returns the second restaurant's information including address
  • Dalton is satisfied with the location of the restaurant

Frequency of Use:3 Times a week
