Casual user persona:

  • Site: Thatswhatcheesesaid.io
  • Name: Raymond Jones
  • Gender: Male
  • Age: 32
  • Technology: Raymond has a IPhone 8 from AT&T, he doesn't keep up with the newest phones, because his IPhone 8 still works fine. He is running the latest OS on his phone. He also has a HP Pavilion, which his work provided him. When not using his work laptop he has an older MSI gaming laptop which he plays Overwatch on. Both of his laptops are running Windows 10.
  • Attitudes: Raymond's favorite food is mac and cheese, he usually reads yelp reviews while determining which restaurant he wants to try next.
  • Needs, Goals and Features: Raymonds wants to be able to see what the top rated mac and cheese in Albuquerque is and also vote on whether he agrees or disagrees.
  • Frustrations: Can’t see written reviews compared to a site like yelp. Doesn’t want to create an account just to vote.

Use case:

  • Title: Down-Forking a restaurant
  • Description: Raymond wants to down-fork a restaurant he recently tried and didn't enjoy.
  • Name of user and role: Raymond’s favorite food is mac and cheese and likes to try new restaurants.
  • Preconditions: Raymond has an account and is signed in.
  • Postconditions: Raymond has down-forked the restaurant he was not happy with and the down-forked number had increased by one.

Interaction flow:

  • 1. Raymond clicks on the restaurant
  • 2. Thatswhatcheesesaid.io brings up that restaurant and displays the amount of up-forks and down-forks
  • 3. Raymond clicks the down-fork button
  • 4. Thatswhatcheesesaid.io takes the button click and increases the total down-forked number by 1
  • 5. Raymonds down-fork is displayed on the total for users to see

Frequency of use:

2-3 times a month (Whenever Raymond is able to try a new restaurant).
