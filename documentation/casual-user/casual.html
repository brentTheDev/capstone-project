Casual user persona:

  • Site: Thatswhatcheesesaid.io
  • Name: Raymond Jones
  • Gender: Male
  • Age: 32
  • Technology: Raymond has a IPhone 8 from AT&T, he doesn't keep up with the newest phones, because his IPhone 8 still works fine. He is running the latest OS on his phone. He also has a HP Pavilion, which his work provided him. When not using his work laptop he has an older MSI gaming laptop which he plays Overwatch on. Both of his laptops are running Windows 10.
  • Attitudes: Raymond's favorite food is mac and cheese, he usually reads yelp reviews while determining which restaurant he wants to try next.
  • Needs, Goals and Features:Raymond, wants to be able to upload his favorite mac and cheese restaurant and up-fork it.
  • Frustrations: Can’t see written reviews compared to a site like yelp.

Use case:

  • Title:Uploading a restaurant and up-forking a restaurant
  • Description:Raymond wants to add a picture of the mac and cheese from his favorite restaurant for users to vote on. He would also like to up-fork that restaurant once uploaded.
  • Name of user and role: Raymond’s favorite food is mac and cheese and has found a new favorite mac and cheese restaurant.
  • Preconditions: Raymond already has an account and is signed in.
  • Postconditions: Raymond uploaded a picture of the his favorite mac and cheese and has up-forked it, increasing the total number by 1.

Interaction flow:

  • 1. Raymond clicks upload button on site
  • 2. The site brings up a page asking to upload a picture and name of the restaurant
  • 3. Raymond selects a picture of the mac and cheese from his saved pictures
  • 4. The site loads Raymond's picture and displays it
  • 5. Raymond enters the restaurants name and hits done.
  • 6. The site takes the information Raymond entered and uploads it the the mac and cheese ranker list and redirects Raymond back to the home page.
  • 7. Raymond sees the restaurant he uploaded on the ranker list and clicks the up-fork button
  • 8. The site recognizes the button click and increases the total up-fork number by 1
  • 9. Raymonds up-fork is displayed on the total for users to see

Frequency of use:

2-3 times a week (Raymond wants to make sure his favorite restaurant is the best).
